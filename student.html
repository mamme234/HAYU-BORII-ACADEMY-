The Problems I Solved - My Journey as a Developer

👋 Hello, I'm Muhammad Ilyas. Let me tell you why I built Hayu Bori Academy.


🔴 The Problem That Started It All: In 2024, I visited a small school in Addis Ababa. I saw teachers manually writing student records in dusty notebooks. ID cards were handwritten on paper that got lost within weeks. Exam cheating was rampant because there was no proctoring system. Parents had no way to check their child's progress. The director couldn't track payments. Teachers applied with paper forms that got lost in drawers. I knew I had to build something better.


⚙️ Problem #1 - No Centralized Student Management → ✅ Digital profiles with Ethiopian ID, photo, grade tracking, exam scores, payments.

🔒 Problem #2 - Exam Cheating & No Security → ✅ Ultra-secure exam: camera capture, no tab switching, violation tracking, 3 strikes termination.

🪪 Problem #3 - Fake / Lost ID Cards → ✅ Digital ID cards with Ethiopian flag, downloadable PNG, printable.

💰 Problem #4 - Payment Confusion & Lost Receipts → ✅ Digital receipts, email confirmations, payment dashboard.

👨‍🏫 Problem #5 - Teacher Application Chaos → ✅ Digital registration, document upload, board approval workflow.

👪 Problem #6 - Parents Had No Visibility → ✅ Parent portal with child's grades, payments, teacher assignments.

📊 Problem #7 - Directors Had No Overview → ✅ Separate dashboards for KG, Elementary, High School directors.

📧 Problem #8 - No Digital Communication → ✅ Real Telegram notifications for registration, exam results, Student IDs, approval codes, payment receipts.


🌟 My Mission Continues: I will keep improving Hayu Bori Academy. It's free, secure, built for Ethiopia.

Privacy Policy - Your Data is Safe With Me

Last Updated: June 2026


📌 What Data Does Hayu Bori Academy Collect?

  • Students: Full Name, Ethiopian ID, email address, phone number, grade level, parent/guardian information, home address, photograph, exam scores, violation records, payment history, Telegram username.
  • Teachers: Full Name, email address, phone number, education documents, teaching grades, subject specialization, years of experience, photograph, exam scores, application status, Telegram username.
  • Parents: Only viewing access to their child's data - no separate data collection.

🔐 How Is Your Data Protected?

  • ✅ All passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing - never stored in plain text.
  • ✅ Exam photos are captured live and only used for identity verification during the exam.
  • ✅ All data is stored in secure MongoDB databases with access controls.
  • ✅ No third-party access - ever. Your data never leaves our secure servers.
  • ✅ HTTPS encryption protects all data transmission.

📸 Exam Proctoring Privacy: Your photo is captured and used ONLY to verify your identity during the exam. Not shared, sold, or used for any other purpose.

👨‍👩‍👧 Parental Rights: Parents have full viewing access to their child's records. Cannot modify data.

🗑️ Data Deletion: Request deletion by emailing privacy@hayuboriacademy.edu.et. Removed within 7 business days.

I built this system with integrity. Your trust matters more than anything.
